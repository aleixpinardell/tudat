Pre-required Knowledge
======================

These pages of the wiki will help you build a strong knowledge basis to get started with Tudat. It is mandatory to understand the concepts taught here before proceding any further. Such knowledge mostly concerns the use of the :literal:`Eigen` and :literal:`boost` external libraries, which support the development using Tudat.
